Quantum Computing Workshop 2024

Learn the Fundamentals of Quantum Computing

The "Quantum Computing Basics & Applications" workshop is designed for beginners in the field. Participants will gain insights into quantum mechanics in computing, including qubits, superposition, entanglement, and quantum algorithms. The workshop explores quantum computing's implications in cryptography, cybersecurity, optimization, and quantum key distribution.

Organized by the Centre for Computational Mathematics, CUTM, Odisha in collaboration with the School of Applied Sciences & Dept. of CSE, SoET. Scheduled from 25th to 27th July 2024, with Dr. Harish Sahu, Scientist-E, DRDO, Delhi as the Resource Person.

Topics Covered

Q Bits, Q Gates, and Q Circuits

Understand the fundamentals of quantum bits, logic gates, and circuits.

Q Entanglement and Q Teleportation

Explore the concepts of quantum entanglement and quantum teleportation.

Q Algorithms and Applications I

Learn about various quantum algorithms and their applications.

Q Algorithms and Applications II

Further delve into advanced quantum algorithms and their practical uses.

Q Error Detection and Correction

Understand the techniques used in quantum error detection and correction.

Quantum Computing Workshop 2024

Learn the Fundamentals of Quantum Computing

Centre for Computational Mathematics, CUTM, Odisha in collaboration with the School of Applied Sciences & Dept. of CSE, SoET is organizing a Workshop on “Quantum Computing” for the students of B.Tech.(CSE/ECE/EEE) & BCA from 25th to 27th July 2024. Dr. Harish Sahu, Scientist-E, DRDO, Delhi is invited as the Resource Person to conduct the workshop.

The "Quantum Computing Basics & Applications" workshop is designed for beginners in the field, providing a foundation to understand the core principles that distinguish quantum computing from classical computing. Participants will learn the fundamentals of quantum mechanics in computing, including qubits, superposition, entanglement, and quantum algorithms. The workshop will also explore the implications of quantum computing in fields such as cryptography, cyber security, optimization, and quantum key distribution.

Topics Covered

Q Bits, Q Gates, and Q Circuits

Understand the fundamentals of quantum bits, logic gates, and circuits.

Q Entanglement and Q Teleportation

Explore the concepts of quantum entanglement and quantum teleportation.

Q Algorithms and Applications I

Learn about various quantum algorithms and their applications.

Q Algorithms and Applications II

Further delve into advanced quantum algorithms and their practical uses.

Q Error Detection and Correction

Understand the techniques used in quantum error detection and correction.
